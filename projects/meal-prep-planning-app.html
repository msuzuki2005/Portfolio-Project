App Design

Meal Prep Planning App

A mobile-first tool that helps busy students plan weekly meals, build grocery lists, and stay on schedule with prep reminders.

Duration
4 weeks
Role
UI/UX Designer
Tools
Figma · React · Netlify
View live site
Hero image

Meal Prep Planning App was designed to reduce the friction of weekly meal planning for students and young professionals who want to eat well without spending hours deciding what to cook.

Challenge

Users struggled to coordinate recipes, ingredients, and prep time across the week. Existing apps felt cluttered or required too much manual input before delivering value.

Process

  1. 1 Research
  2. 2 Define
  3. 3 Ideate
  4. 4 Prototype
  5. 5 Test

Research

Interviewed 6 users about meal planning habits and pain points.

Define

Mapped user journeys and prioritized a weekly planner flow.

Ideate

Sketched layout options for calendar, list, and recipe views.

Prototype

Built clickable prototypes for usability testing.

Sketching

Early explorations focused on simplifying the weekly calendar and making grocery lists feel automatic rather than tedious.

Reflection

This project strengthened my ability to balance feature depth with a calm, approachable interface. User testing revealed that grocery list automation was the highest-value moment in the flow.

If I continued development, I would explore recipe import integrations and smarter prep-time estimates based on user history.
