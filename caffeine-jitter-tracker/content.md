## Overview

Caffeine Jitter Tracker started as a personal project to make sense of coffee habits during a busy semester. The app turns quick logs into a visual daily summary.

## Challenge

Caffeine tracking tools often feel clinical or guilt-driven. The goal was a friendly interface that encourages awareness without shame.

## Solution

One-tap logging for common drinks paired with a daily bar chart and hourly breakdown powered by Chart.js.

## Outcome

The tracker uses local storage for offline logging. Warning states pair color with text to meet accessibility requirements.
