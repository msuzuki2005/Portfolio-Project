## Overview

Verdure was created for plant owners who love their greenery but struggle to remember watering schedules. The app combines gentle reminders with a visual plant library and care history.

## Challenge

Plant care apps often feel clinical or overwhelming. Users needed a calm, encouraging experience that adapts to different plant types without requiring expert knowledge upfront.

## Solution

The interface focuses on today's care tasks with quick actions for water, mist, and rotate. Onboarding was simplified to three steps: add plant, set schedule, receive reminders.

## Outcome

Peer feedback sessions showed that softer visual rhythm and supportive copy made users feel encouraged rather than nagged. The prototype is ready for further development.
