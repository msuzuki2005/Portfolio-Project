UX Design

Verdure

A plant watering app that helps users track care schedules, monitor plant health, and build consistent routines for thriving houseplants.

View live site
Duration
6 weeks
Role
Lead Designer
Tools
Figma · React · Tailwind
Hero image

Verdure was created for plant owners who love their greenery but struggle to remember watering schedules. The app combines gentle reminders with a visual plant library and care history.

Challenge

Plant care apps often feel clinical or overwhelming. Users needed a calm, encouraging experience that adapts to different plant types without requiring expert knowledge upfront.

Process

  1. 1 Research
  2. 2 Define
  3. 3 Ideate
  4. 4 Prototype
  5. 5 Test

Research

Surveyed plant owners and analyzed competitor onboarding flows.

Define

Defined personas around casual collectors and first-time plant parents.

Ideate

Explored dashboard layouts centered on upcoming care tasks.

Prototype

Tested reminder timing and plant profile setup with 5 participants.

Sketching

Initial sketches explored a home screen focused on today's tasks, with quick actions for water, mist, and rotate.

Reflection

Verdure taught me how much emotional tone matters in utility apps. Small copy changes and softer visual rhythm made users feel supported rather than nagged.

Future iterations could add plant identification via camera and community care tips from experienced growers.
