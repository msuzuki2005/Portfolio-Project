Mia Suzuki

I'm a graphic design student exploring the space where visual craft meets code — building thoughtful digital experiences one project at a time.

My work is driven by clarity, structure, and a love for systems that feel as good as they look. Outside the studio, you'll find me in marching band or chasing down new tools to learn.

Work experience

At Rippke Design, I work with a team of designers in a fast paced environment to complete projects for clients across the nation in various fields and industries.

As one of the designers with the MSS (Multicultural Student Success) and BUILD organizations, I collaborate with a team of designers to create marketing materials and promotional publications used to advertise events and workshops.

Working with the Story County regional director and Iowa 4-H programs I designed flyers, brochures, and booklets to market 4-H clubs and gardening/nutritional programming. I also worked with the Story County Fairboard to help improve fairgrounds and designed the map and schedule brochure that was used for the 2025 Story County Fair.

During my time with BUILD, I mentored first year design students and guided them through their time in the core program. If they had any questions about any classes, their assignments/projects, or needed advice on their work, I was there as their mentor to give them any and all support that they needed.

I work with clients throughout the year to develop custom branding, logos, marketing assets, and more, managing projects from concept to final delivery. I collaborate closely with each client to understand their vision and goals.
