## Overview

Meal Prep Planning App was designed to reduce the friction of weekly meal planning for students and young professionals who want to eat well without spending hours deciding what to cook.

## Challenge

Users struggled to coordinate recipes, ingredients, and prep time across the week. Existing apps felt cluttered or required too much manual input before delivering value.

## Solution

The design centers on a weekly planner with auto-generated grocery lists and gentle prep reminders. Low-fidelity wireframes were tested before high-fidelity screens were built.

## Outcome

User testing confirmed that grocery list automation was the highest-value moment in the flow. The prototype was deployed on Netlify for portfolio presentation.
